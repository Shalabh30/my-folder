Steps to make a Cheese Sandwitch

  1. Get Bread
  2. Get Cheese
  3. Get Plates
  4. Get Knife
  5. Keep Bread on Plate
  6. Take Some Cheese on Knife
  7. Spread Cheese on First Bread
  8. Place Second Bread on First Bread
  9. Press Bread2 against Bread1
  10. Put Bread in Toaster
  11. Takeout Bread From Toaster and place it on Plate
  12. Chees Sandwitch is ready :)
